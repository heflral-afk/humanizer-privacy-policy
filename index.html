Privacy Policy for Muddle: Final Draft Humanizer

Effective date: April 5, 2026

Developer: hefIral-afk

Muddle: Final Draft Humanizer helps users rewrite text using AI. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Information we collect

We may collect and store an app-generated account or device identifier, authentication or session data, subscription and purchase records, including purchase tokens, product IDs, entitlement status, token balances, and renewal or expiry information, and limited technical or usage information needed for security, fraud prevention, troubleshooting, and app operation.

Text you submit

When you submit text in Muddle: Final Draft Humanizer, your text is sent through our backend and transmitted to our AI processing provider so the app can generate a rewritten result.

We do not store your submitted original text or rewritten output in our database after processing, except where temporary processing is technically necessary or where retention is required by law.

How we use information

We use information to provide text rewriting features, verify subscriptions and token purchases, maintain entitlement status and token balances, prevent fraud, abuse, and replay attacks, and secure, maintain, and improve the app.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited data with service providers that help operate the app, such as AI processing providers, Google Play or related payment and platform providers for purchase verification, and hosting, infrastructure, and security providers.

Data retention

We retain account, authentication, purchase, entitlement, and related security records for as long as needed to operate the app, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and prevent fraud.

Submitted text is processed transiently and is not retained in our database after processing, except where short-term handling is technically necessary.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect your information.

Your choices

You may contact us to request account or data deletion, subject to legal, fraud-prevention, and transaction-record retention requirements.

Children

Muddle: Final Draft Humanizer is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the revised effective date.
